- SimPy es una libreria de simulación de eventos discretos
- The behavior of active components (like vehicles, customers or messages) is modeled with _processes_.
- All processes live in an _environment_.
- They interact with the environment and with each other via _events_.


An important event type is the [`Timeout`](https://simpy.readthedocs.io/en/latest/api_reference/simpy.events.html#simpy.events.Timeout "simpy.events.Timeout").
