## Brief history of AI
- 1950s Birth of AI
	- Alan Turing proposes the Turing Test
	- John McCarthy coins "Artificial Intelligence"
- 1970s Expert Systems
	- MYCIN: AI Rule-based system to detect bacteria
- 1990s Machine Learning of Data Mining
	- Computers became more powerful
	- Collect Data, Organize, Analize
	- Statistical and Mathematical  breaktrhoughs
- 1997 Deep Blue
	- IBM's Deep Blue defeats world chess champion Garry Kasparov
- 2010s Deep Learning Revolution
	- Google's AlphaGo defeating Go champion Lee Sedol in 2016
	- More difficult than Chess
- 2020s AI in Everyday Life
	- Virtual assistants
	- Autonomous vehicles
	- Healtchare Diagnostics
	- Discussions on ethics and regulations
