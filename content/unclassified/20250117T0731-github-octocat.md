# Github Octocat
## Concepto Principal
**GitHub's Octocat** is the platform's **official mascot**.
- The character is a hybrid of an **octopus** and a **cat**.
- It symbolizes **GitHub's** friendly and engaging nature in the world of software development and collaboration.
## Contexto
- You can **create your own Octocat** by visiting [https://myoctocat.com](https://myoctocat.com/).
## Aspectos Clave
- **Octocat** represents GitHub's **friendly** community.
- The mascot combines elements of an **octopus** and a **cat** to highlight its adaptability and creativity.
- **myOctocat** allows users to personalize their version of **Octocat**, enhancing community engagement.
