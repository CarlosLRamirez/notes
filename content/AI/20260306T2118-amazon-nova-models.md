## Main Concept 
Amazon Nova is a family of base models (FM) owned by AWS and available through Amazon Bedrock. They were launched in 2023 and are AWS's new bet to replace the Amazon Titan family.

## Context
Amazon Nova models are the replacement of Amazon Titan Models
## Amazon Nova models family
- **Nova Premier**
	- The most capable model, for complex reasoning tasks
	- Multimodal
	- The best teacher for distilling custom models.
- **Nova Pro**
	- A highly capable model with the best combination of accuracy, speed and cost
	- Multimodal
	- For a wide range of tasks
- **Nova Lite**
	- Very low-cost, very fast for processing images, video and text inputs
	- Multimodal
- **Nova Micro**
	- Text-only
	- The lowest latency reponse at vey low cost.
- **Nova Canva**
	- For image generation
- **Nova Reel**
	- For video generation
- **Nova Sonic**
	- Conversational speech understanding and generation
	- Multiple Languages

> [!NOTE]
> The AIF-C01 exam guide was written when Titan was the predominant AWS model. Nova was released later. The exam will likely mention Titan more than Nova — but both may appear.
