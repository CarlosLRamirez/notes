## Main Concept 
The base models (FM) of the Amazon Nova 2 family are the newest from AWS, complementing the existing Amazon Nova models. It has enhanced capabilities for building complex AI applications.
## Context
The Nova 2 models are an expansion of the Nova family, an iteration of the same family with improved models.
## Key Aspects
- They can have up to 1M context tokens and advanced reasoning capabilities.
## Modelos Nova 2
- **Nova 2 Lite:** fast, cost-effective reasoning model for **everyday workloads**, multimodal.
- **Nova 2 Sonic:** Speech-to-Speech FM, for natural, real-time voice conversations (speech, text).
- **Nova 2 Multimodal Embeddings:** for agentic RAG and semantic search apps.
- **Nova 2 Omni:** all-in-one model for multimodal reasoning and image generation.
