## Core Definition:
AI applications span domains where computational intelligence solves problems, automates tasks, or augments human capability. Use cases vary by maturity: some are production-ready, others experimental.

## Key Application Categories:

- _Business & Operations:_ Customer service (chatbots), process automation (RPA), supply chain optimization, predictive maintenance, fraud detection, pricing optimization
- _Creative & Content:_ Text generation, image synthesis, music composition, video editing, code generation, design assistance
- _Healthcare:_ Diagnostic imaging, drug discovery, personalized treatment plans, patient monitoring, clinical documentation
- _Finance:_ Algorithmic trading, risk assessment, credit scoring, portfolio management, compliance monitoring
- _Transportation & Logistics:_ Autonomous vehicles, route optimization, predictive dispatch, fleet management
- _Education:_ Personalized learning, tutoring systems, automated grading, content recommendation
- _Research & Science:_ Data analysis, pattern recognition, hypothesis generation, materials discovery, genomics
## Maturity Spectrum:

- **Deployed**: Customer service, recommendation systems, predictive analytics
- **Emerging**: Autonomous systems, creative AI, medical diagnosis support
- **Experimental**: AGI applications, novel scientific discovery
