## Main Concept

Large Languate Models or LLMs are a specific type of [[20251029T060940-foundation-model|Foundation Model]] specialized to generate coherent human-like text and are capable to perform language-related task, like translation, summarization, question answering, content creation.

## Context

- LLMs are the models behind popular chatbots like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, which have made AI mainstream.

## Key Aspects

- LLMs are trained using a large _"corpus"_ of text data, which means a large collections of text documents, like books, articles, websites, newspapers, and other text data
- LLMs are very big models, with billions of parameters
- LLMs are behind not only chatbots but also other type of tools that requires human language type of interaction.
